## How to Use Prompts

1. Click "AI Theme Generator" in the theme selector in the top right corner
2. Enter your desired theme description, or click on recommended prompts
3. AI will automatically generate the corresponding theme configuration
4. Preview the effect and apply it to your project

## Recommended Prompts

### Natural Style

- **Warm Sunny** Sunny orange primary tone, creating a warm and sunny atmosphere, giving a positive and uplifting feeling
- **Fresh Forest Green** Natural green tones, creating a fresh and natural feeling, suitable for health and eco-friendly products
- **Ocean Azure** Blue primary tones, bringing the depth and tranquility of the ocean
- **Sunset Glow** Warm color gradients, simulating the cozy ambiance of dusk
- **Cherry Blossom Spring** Soft pink-purple tones, bringing the romantic feeling of cherry blossoms

### Business Style

- **Professional Business Blue** Steady blue tones, showcasing a professional and reliable business image
- **Business Elite Black** Dark tones with gold accents, highlighting premium quality
- **Minimalist Business Gray** Neutral gray tones, simple yet professional
- **FinTech Style** Tech blue gradients combined with steady dark elements
- **Modern Dark Business** Dark theme design, suitable for professional business scenarios

- **Japanese Natural Wood** Warm wood tones, creating a natural and simple Japanese style

### Playful Style

### Brand Customization

Generate themes based on brand colors. Provide hex codes or describe brand personality:

- "My brand color is #E91E63, I want a pink theme"
- "We're a tech startup, need a modern blue theme"
- "Education product, want warm orange tones that feel friendly"

## Tips for Writing Great Prompts

### Be Specific with Colors

✅ Recommended: "Use deep blue as primary color with white background" ❌ Avoid: "Make it blue"

### Describe the Use Case

✅ Recommended: "For a fintech backend management system" ❌ Avoid: "For a management system"

### Describe User Feelings

✅ Recommended: "Give users a sense of professional trust" ❌ Avoid: "Make it look good"

### Comprehensive Description

✅ Recommended: "For healthcare applications, use fresh green tones to create a healthy and reliable feeling"

### Specify Contrast Needs

✅ Recommended: "Need high contrast for easy reading" ✅ Recommended: "Soft tones to reduce visual fatigue"

## FAQ

### What language can be used for prompts?

It is recommended to use Chinese or English for prompts to get the best results.

### Can generated themes be modified?

Yes. Click "Theme Editor" to further adjust the generated theme configuration.

### How to save the generated theme?

In the Theme Editor, click the "Save" button to save the theme configuration locally.

### How many recommended prompts are available?

Every time you generate, the system shows 6 recommended prompts. You can click to use them directly or enter your own description.
